Frequently Asked Questions

How does GS3 source for products?
GS3 sources for products through various means and arrangements. For regulated products like PMS, we source from PPMC via the Bulk Purchase Agreement (Coastal BPA), direct purchase or JV with major marketers and private depot owners like AA Rano, AYM Shafa, Nipco, Emadeb. Once products are obtained and are in depot storage, they are offered for sale to registered members of the Independent Petroleum Marketers (IPMAN) on GS3.
What products can I order on GS3?
The only products you can order on GS3 are petrol, diesel, kerosene, and LPG.
