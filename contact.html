Frequently Asked Questions

How does GS3 source for products?
GS3 sources for products through various means and arrangements. For regulated products like PMS, we source from PPMC via the Bulk Purchase Agreement (Coastal BPA), direct purchase or JV with major marketers and private depot owners like AA Rano, AYM Shafa, Nipco, Emadeb. Once products are obtained and are in depot storage, they are offered for sale to registered members of the Independent Petroleum Marketers (IPMAN) on GS3.
What products can I order on GS3?
The only products you can order on GS3 are petrol, diesel, kerosene, and LPG.
How can I get my order delivered to my retail outlet?
There are two options to get your products to you. We have the ‘self loading’ option where you provide your truck to pick up products from the loading depots while the second option is to use our delivery partners, ‘BridgeMe’. BridgeMe will provide the trucks and drivers needed to transport your order to you.
Does GS3 operate nationwide?
Yes, GS3 operations are available nationwide
Can I order products on credit?
Yes, you can. However, only registered business owners can access our credit facility. As a Business Owner, you will need to apply to get credit from your dashboard. Your documents will then be verified by our experts and you’ll be allowed to order for products on credit.
What is the estimated delivery time?
If you choose to use BridgeMe, delivery will approximately be between 24 to 72 hours, depending on the destination, as well as the time of pickup.
